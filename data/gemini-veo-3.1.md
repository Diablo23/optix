Veo 3.1: The Cinematic Director

Veo 3.1 handles 8-second high-fidelity video with native audio. You must prompt like a director on a soundstage.

The Directorial Prompt Order
Camera: (Movement/Angle) "Dolly-in shot towards..."

Subject: (Description) "A weathered explorer..."

Action: (Pacing) "...slowly uncovering a glowing artifact."

Setting: (Environment/Lighting) "A dark, damp cave lit only by a pulsating blue light."

Audio: (Soundscape) "Include the sound of dripping water and a low humming synth."

Consistency Strategy
Reference Frames: Use the "First and Last Frame" feature. Generate your starting frame with Nano Banana Pro, then upload it to Veo to ensure your characters don't "morph" during the video.

Timestamp Prompting: For complex 8-second clips, you can specify actions: "0-4s: Character looks up in awe; 4-8s: Character reaches out a trembling hand."
