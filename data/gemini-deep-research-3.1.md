Deep Research 3.1 Pro: The Autonomous Agent

Deep Research is not a chatbot; it is an agent that plans and executes. It works best when you give it a "Research Blueprint" rather than a single question.

The "3-Step Workflow"
Plan: Ask the model to generate a research plan first.

Refine: Review the plan and add missing keywords or specific sources (e.g., "Look for 2025 financial reports").

Execute: Give the final "Go" to produce the report.

Pro Tips
Keyword Injection: Deep Research uses your prompt to generate search queries. Include specific brands, technical terms, or dates (e.g., "ASML High-NA EUV lithography 2026 specs").

Constraint of Unknowns: Tell it how to handle missing data: "If specific 2025 figures are unavailable, state they are projections; do not estimate."
